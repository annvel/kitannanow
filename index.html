BIO

You know that one friend who floats around life with a beaming smile, doesn't get choosy with the life experience? Yeah, that's Kitanna. She was born and raised in Meru in Kenya and she thanks her neighbourhood for all she is. When they say don't forget where you came from, Kitanna not left behind. She has a philathropic spirit besides her angelic voice. One thing you bet you didn't know about her is that she has raised over Ksh. 1.5 million in under 2 months to help children access quality education as she thanks her mother, who by the way was windowed when she was only five, for giving her a chance in life and enabling her spread her wings and that is why she is here today. You want to know more about her and her unique perspective she brings to the world of music, then you are in the right place. For more, follow her social media @kitannanow to see what her day to day life looks like and also to have a glimpse of behind the scenes of her creating her art.
